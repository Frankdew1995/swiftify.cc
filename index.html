Swiftify - Better software for gastronomy industry

Discover more

Swiftify is a versatile and multifunctional desktop application built for efficient restaurant management, which implements built-in QR-Code dish ordering, cashier system, tax-compliance, menu management, and data analytics.

Explore the product

Features

Easy to use

The application runs completely standalone from your windows PC and requires no wifi or network.


Mobile-friendly

Your staff visits the application from mobile devices by scanning the QR Code to check out the guest bills or update existing guest orders etc. Less time running back and forth for your POS or cashier system desk, more time for service to your guests.


QR-Code ordering

Your guests order directly by scanning the QR Code and simultaneously the details of ordered items are pushed and printed to the different kitchen departments by our programmatically-controlled printers. Fewer guest frustrations caused by human errors, more culinary joyfulness to your guests.

Smart Printing

By taking orders via mobile ordering, ordered items will be sorted and allocated by categories (drinks or food) and transmitted via printing to your restaurant's corresponding departments (bar or kitchen). This will ensure accurate, efficient collaboration and communication between your service staff and kitchen.


Data analytics

Gain insights via generated real-time data analytics and visualization from the admin dashboard. No more gut judging and make more data-driven decisions to drive more sales and improve the guest experience for your restaurants.


Online Food Delivery Store

You can activate and start using the built-in online delivery food store with just one click, which can be used as one online food ordering store for guests that are not visiting your store or you can easily port this to a vending machine that has a user interface for in-house guests that would like to order take-outs.


Staff Management

You can easily assign different accounts and privileges for your staff such as cashier, waiter, store manager or store owner. Staff management is easy than ever before!

Secure & tax compliance

You can easily generate and print out the daily tax-compliant z-financial reporting sheet (Z-Bericht in German) and submit it to your local tax authorities. All your staff operational activities and logs will be saved in a secure local database and backed up in our cloud storage for financial legal obligatory purposes. Never compromise on security and compliance again!
